KEY FINDINGS — DATASET2
==============================================================================

1. REENTRANCY DETECTION: SVS excels at detecting reentrancy through
   postcondition violations (4/4 TP, 100% recall on vulnerable functions).
   CVL misses reentrancy entirely (3 FN) due to single-transaction model,
   but catches function-can-revert issues (5 TP of different nature).

2. SAFE CONTRACT VERIFICATION: Both tools struggle with safe contracts.
   SVS: 50% TN rate, CVL: 30% TN rate. External calls, loops, and
   complex math are the main FP sources. This is the largest contributor
   to low precision for both tools.

3. TIME MANIPULATION: CVL has higher recall (5/5 = 100%) vs SVS (4/6 = 67%).
   SVS misses timestamp-based drain in complex contracts with inheritance
   (HotDollarsToken, TTC).

4. PERFORMANCE: SVS is 4.10x faster overall. Speedup ranges from 1.47x
   (gasless_send) to 6.24x (unchecked_send).

5. NEW PATTERNS vs DATASET1:
   - SVS INTERNAL ERROR on TokenSender (new error type)
   - CVL FN on reentrancy (not seen in dataset1)
   - safecontract benchmark reveals verification limitations for both tools
